A micro department store located on Chicago's Magnificent Mile that's anything but general.

From furniture to apothecary, space519's inventory is ever changing. For a small business, sharing new products and connecting with customers online tends to be a challege. My job was to better inform their customers and increase brand awareness. I started by updated their existing Wordpress site to represent their overall branding and created new posts on a weekly basis.
Check out the live site here.


I helped promote marketing campaigns including pop-up-shops, limited releases, and seasonal events. Using tools like Sprout Social, we measured engagment analytics and gain insights on the success of each deploy. To improve communication on multiple social media channels, I worked with the store's sales team to use these networks as a customer support tool and deliver high quality, engaging, channel-specific content on a frequent basis.


I designed a custom themed email template featuring new products and store events. Using email marketing strategies such as A/B testing and list segmentation, we built a 1K+ engaged email house list with an open rate double the industry average. With a branded iPad app at checkout, we captured emails at time of sale. This allowed us a direct channel to interact with our customers and deliver new product updates.

Closet.Wardrobe.You.

Responsive One-Page Website

View Company Website

Scrolling can be used in an awesome way to help convey a message or tell a story. I spent a few weeks designing and developing a fully responsive one-page site for Shellie Thompson, a talented Chicago based full-service stylist.

Her previous website was a Wix site that was cluttered and difficult to navigate. To better meet the needs of her business, a one-page design approach was chosen that prioritizes screen size, message, and context. She wanted a website to communicate her personal brand, educate customers, and cross-promote social. The main focus of this project was sharing core service offerings and making it easy to discover what Closet.Wardrobe.You. is about.

Final Design

The final website was built with a responsive framework optimized for mobile. By making sure that it was visible on a variety of screen sizes, the website was both accessible and progressive. I worked with Shellie on the copy and image choices.

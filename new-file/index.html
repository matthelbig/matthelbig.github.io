Who We Are

Foster Hill Design is an award-winning, remodeling and interior design firm.

With projects completed nationwide, our firm is based in Chicago.

Philosophy

We believe in Livable Modern -- spaces with clean lines and high function, classic materials combined with new, and warm environs layered with pattern and texture. Our mission is to create beautiful, livable spaces that meet our clients’ aesthetic and functional needs.

Approach

We work with some clients who have an idea of what they want and we help them achieve it. For others, they need help to realize what’s possible in their space and we help them see it.

Services

Services range from complete interior decorating including space planning, upholstery and furniture selection to new construction, full home remodels, kitchen and bath design and architectural and cabinetry design.

Nicole understands the importance of client creative collaboration, providing premier client service and unparalleled attention to detail. She honed these skills during a fifteen year career as a VP, Advertising Director leading senior-level business clientele. Her design firm makes the entire design process turn-key for her clients, resulting in over 90% of new business stemming from existing clients and referrals. Prior to starting her own interior design firm, Nicole apprenticed at Nate Berkus Associates and attended Harrington College of Design for Interior Design specific studies, in addition to a BA from Miami University of Ohio.

Michele earned her bachelors degree in Interior Design from Harrington College of Design, where she was a Harrington College Service and Leadership scholar. Prior to signing on with Foster Hill Design, she apprenticed with Morgante Wilson Architects and later developed a collaborative in Chicago working with a diverse group of leading designers. In this role, she assisted with full-service design planning from conceptual and architectural plans development through to final execution and installation. Her collaborative work with diverse designers gave her a breadth of experience spanning design aesthetics.

Express Pigeon

Mobile Email Templates

View Company Website

While working with TreeRock, I was introduced to Igor and his email design startup Express Pigeon. Being relatively new to responsive mobile email design, the opportunity to work on a few modern email templates to be introduced to the templates library was awesome.

We spoke a few times about the goals of the templates and the future vision of the email delivery platform overall. While the templates had a single-column focus, we wanted to test some responsive multi-column layouts. Keeping in mind that these templates would need to both communicate potential use cases and be easily customized by users, I got started creating a few versions. The end result was three mobile friendly live templates.
